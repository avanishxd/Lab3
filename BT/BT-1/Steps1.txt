Q. Installation of MetaMask and Study of Spending Ether per Transaction

Steps:
1. Go to https://metamask.io
2. Click on "Get MetaMask"
3. Choose "Add Extension to Chrome" (or your preferred browser)
4. Once installed, click on the MetaMask icon in your browser toolbar
5. Click "Get Started" → "Create a New Wallet"
6. Follow on-screen instructions to set up your wallet
7. Create a strong password
8. Securely store your Secret Recovery Phrase (Seed Phrase)
9. Your MetaMask Wallet is now created successfully

Q. To study Ether spending per transaction:
1. Click on your wallet account
2. Go to "Networks" → Turn on "Show Test Networks"
3. Select "Sepolia Test Network"
4. Open a new browser tab → Search for "Sepolia Faucet Google Cloud"
5. Paste your wallet’s Public Address in the faucet input box
6. Click "Receive" or "Request Funds"
7. Your wallet will now receive 0.05 SepoliaETH
8. Go back to MetaMask and confirm the balance
9. Perform a small transaction (send Ether to another account)
10. After the transaction, open Activity → Transaction Details

Study and note down:
1. Gas Limit
2. Gas Price
3. Total Fee (ETH spent per transaction)
