Q. Getting Test ETH:

1. Open MetaMask and go to Networks settings.
2. Enable "Show test networks".
3. Select Sepolia test network.
4. Visit a Sepolia faucet (e.g., Google Cloud Faucet).
5. Paste your wallet's public address.
6. Click "Receive" to claim 0.05 SepoliaETH.

Q. Testing Transactions:
1. Create a second account in MetaMask.
2. Send SepoliaETH from your first account to the second.
3. Review transaction details: gas limit, gas price, and total cost.
4. Check the transaction history for confirmation.
